About GRIT Events
Who we are, what we believe, and why we exist.
Our Story

GRIT Events was founded in Nairobi, Kenya in 2024 with a singular, powerful belief — that extraordinary events do not happen by accident. They are built, piece by piece, through discipline, creativity, and an unrelenting refusal to accept anything less than the best.

Led by founder and lead event planner Grace Titus, GRIT Events was born from a deep passion for creating experiences that move people — events that guests remember not just for how they looked, but for how they felt.

Based in Nairobi, we serve clients across Kenya, East Africa, and internationally — from intimate family ceremonies to high-profile corporate summits with hundreds of delegates.
