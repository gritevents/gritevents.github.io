Our Story

GRIT Event Planners was founded in Nairobi, Kenya in 2024 with one clear purpose: to bring genuine professional discipline to event planning in East Africa. We exist to be the strategic partner that clients trust to manage every element of their most important events — from the first planning session to the final handshake.

Led by founder Grace Titus, GRIT Event Planners was built on the conviction that events are not just occasions — they are strategic opportunities to communicate, connect, celebrate, and create lasting impressions. They deserve to be planned with intention and executed with precision.

We serve corporate organizations, government entities, and private clients across Nairobi, Kenya, East Africa, and internationally.

Our Core Role

GRIT Event Planners operates as the strategic planner, project manager, and operational coordinator of every event we take on. We are not a décor company or a styling vendor. We are the professional body that brings every element of your event together — planning the vision, building the team, managing the logistics, and ensuring flawless delivery.

"Think of us as the architect and project manager of your event — we design the plan, assemble the best specialists, and manage everything through to completion."
