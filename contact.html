Questions We

How far in advance should I book GRIT Events? +
For weddings and large corporate events, we recommend booking at least 3–6 months in advance to ensure availability and allow sufficient planning time. For smaller events, 4–8 weeks is usually workable. That said, we will always try to accommodate urgent requests — contact us and we will do our best.
Do you provide a quotation before I commit? +
Absolutely. Our process always begins with a free consultation where we understand your vision and requirements. We then provide a detailed, written quotation — with no obligation to proceed. We believe in full transparency before any commitment is made.
Do you work with all budget ranges? +
We work with a range of budgets — but we are always honest about what is achievable. GRIT Events specializes in luxury events, and we will always tell you clearly what is possible within your budget rather than over-promising and under-delivering. Our goal is that every client feels the full weight of our expertise, regardless of scale.
Can you plan events outside Nairobi? +
Yes — we plan events across Kenya, East Africa, and internationally. We have managed destination events in Diani, Naivasha, Mombasa, and beyond. For international events, we conduct thorough remote planning with on-ground coordination teams in the destination city. Distance is never a barrier to GRIT Events' standard.
What happens after I send my enquiry? +
Within 2 hours of your enquiry, a member of the GRIT Events team will respond personally. We will then schedule a consultation — either in person in Nairobi or via call/video — where we learn more about your event. From there, we provide a detailed proposal and quotation, and if you are happy to proceed, we begin the planning process officially.
What are your payment terms? +
A 50% non-refundable deposit is required to confirm your booking and begin planning. The balance is due 7 days before your event date. For larger events, we can discuss a phased payment schedule. All payment terms are clearly outlined in your written agreement before any work begins.
Do you handle everything, or do I need to source some vendors myself? +
With our full-service planning package, we handle absolutely everything — including all vendor sourcing, briefing, management, and coordination. You do not need to contact a single supplier yourself. However, if you already have preferred vendors you would like to use, we are happy to incorporate and manage them within our overall coordination.
