Behind The Scenes

The Work You Never See

Every flawless event is built on a foundation of invisible work. Here's what happens long before the guests arrive.

01
☕
The Discovery Meeting

We sit down — with coffee, with questions, and with no rush. We learn everything about your vision, your non-negotiables, and the feeling you want your guests to leave with.

Week 1 · 2–3 hours · Vision Alignment
02
📍
The Site Visit

We walk every metre of the venue. We measure, photograph, assess lighting, test acoustics, and map every constraint. No surprises on event day — ever.

Week 2 · Full-Day Assessment · Documentation
03
🎨
The Design Session

Mood boards, floor plans, colour stories, lighting concepts, floral direction — we build your event's entire visual world on paper before a single item is ordered.

Week 3–4 · Creative Development · Client Review
04
📞
The Vendor Briefings

Every supplier receives a detailed written brief — photos, measurements, timings, expectations. We manage all communication so you never have to chase anyone.

Week 4–8 · Supplier Management · Contracts
Phase 5
The Rehearsal

We run through the full event programme — entrances, transitions, speeches, table service — until every person involved knows exactly what to do and when.

Phase 6
The Setup Day

Our team arrives at dawn. Décor is placed, lighting is rigged, tables are styled, every detail is checked and rechecked. We don't leave until it's perfect.

Phase 7
Your Moment

You walk in. Everything is ready. Our team manages every moment from the background — so you feel nothing but joy, pride, and presence.
