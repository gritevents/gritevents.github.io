⭐ The Visionary · No. 01

Grace Titus

Founder & Lead Event Planner · Nairobi, Kenya

Grace is not just the founder of GRIT Events — she is the living embodiment of everything the brand stands for. Resilient, relentless, and deeply creative, she built GRIT Events from the ground up with a singular belief: that every event, regardless of its size, deserves to be extraordinary.

With an eye for design that borders on obsessive and a work ethic that has been described by her team as "frankly alarming in the best possible way," Grace leads every project with both her heart and her strategy. She is the first to arrive at a venue and the last to leave — and she will notice if a single candle is one centimetre out of place.

"I started GRIT Events because I believe that great events are not a luxury — they are a love language. And I plan to make East Africa fluent in it."
Something You Should Know About Grace

She has been known to rearrange her own living room at midnight — not because anything was wrong, but because the spatial flow "wasn't right." Her team takes this as a feature, not a bug. Also, she will outwork everyone in the room and still look flawless doing it. We are both inspired and slightly intimidated. Mostly inspired.
